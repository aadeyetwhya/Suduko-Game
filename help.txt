
			
The objective is to fill a 9×9 grid with digits in such a way that each
column, each row,and each of the nine 3×3 sub grids (also called "boxes",
"blocks", or "regions") contains all of the digits from 1to 9 and the
digit shouldn't be repeated in its respective row,column and box.
Press 'W' to move up
      'S' to move down
      'A' to move left
      'D' to move right.
